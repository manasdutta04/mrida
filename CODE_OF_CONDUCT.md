# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in MRIDA
a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size,
visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic
status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, religion, or sexual identity.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness
- Respecting different viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback
- Taking responsibility and apologizing when we make mistakes
- Prioritizing what is best for the community and end users

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, insulting language, or personal attacks
- Trolling or deliberate disruption
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Any conduct inappropriate in a professional, community setting

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct.
They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, and other contributions
that are not aligned with this code of conduct.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies in all project spaces and when an individual is
officially representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to the maintainers via the contact method in `SUPPORT.md`.
All reports will be reviewed promptly and fairly.
